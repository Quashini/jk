About

South Devon Websites

We are Totnes-based web developers (also catering for clients outside of Totnes) who are keen to work with freelancers and small & medium-sized businesses to build their presence online. We specialize in WordPress websites, as well as custom coded Bootstrap sites and ecommerce sites. On top of web design, we offer hosting, security, and maintenance to keep your website online, malware free, fast, with built-in spam protection. We also provide legal documentation to keep your website GDPR compliant.

Feel free to contact us for a 30-minute free consultation where we will go through your needs and requirements.
